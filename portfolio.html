Breakout

Breakout is a game based off of the hit game Pong where you battle it out between two people.

In Breakout your goal is to destroy all of the blocks using the ball and paddle and to make

it through all three levels. If you lose all 3 of your lives you lose but if you complete

all 3 levels you win. Good Luck.
